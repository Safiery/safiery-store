01 Scotty AI DC-DC

The AI brain of your DC system

Scotty AI is a bi-directional DC-DC converter that auto-tunes to your alternator in two minutes — no programming. It charges 12, 24, 36 or 48V banks, protects the alternator with real-time thermal AI, and appears natively on your Victron Cerbo.

  • AI auto-tune in two minutes — plug & play
  • 1.5kW & 3kW, fully bi-directional
  • 100% VE.CAN / Victron compliant

  • 10,000 cycles · 5-year warranty
